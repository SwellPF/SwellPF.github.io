Paul has been fascinated by computers since he was a child. His parents bought him a Radio Shack TRS-80 Model I when he was 12. He taught himself BASIC from the manual that came with his computer. Paul has been a programmer, an instructor training business professionals on commercial software and managed the online learning program for one of the largest law firms in the country for nearly 18 years. He lives with his husband and three cats in Fort Myers, Florida.
